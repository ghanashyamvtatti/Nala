# Classic Guacamole
## Description
Simple, fresh, and authentic guacamole.

## Ingredients
- 3 ripe avocados
- 1/2 onion, finely diced
- 2 roma tomatoes, diced
- 3 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced
- 2 limes, juiced
- Salt to taste

## Steps
1. Cut avocados in half, remove pit, and scoop into a bowl.
2. Mash avocados with a fork until desired consistency.
3. Stir in onion, tomatoes, cilantro, jalapeño, and lime juice.
4. Season with salt to taste.

## Additional Information
Serve immediately with tortilla chips. To store, press plastic wrap directly onto the surface to prevent browning.
