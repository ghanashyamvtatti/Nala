# Fluffy Pancakes

## Description
The fluffiest pancakes you'll ever make. Perfect for a Sunday morning breakfast.

## Ingredients
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 tbsp melted butter
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## Steps
1. Whisk dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk wet ingredients.
3. Pour wet ingredients into dry and mix until just combined (lumps are okay!).
4. Heat a griddle or pan over medium heat and grease with butter.
5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
6. Cook until bubbles form on top, then flip and cook until golden brown.

## Variations
- Add blueberries or chocolate chips to the batter.
- Substitute milk with buttermilk for tangier pancakes.
