# Mom's Rasam Powder

## Ingredients
- Byadagi red chillies 250g
- Coriander seeds 250g
- Fenugreek seeds 50g
- Black peppercorns 50g
- Cumin seeds 50g
- Mustard seeds 25g
- Toor dal 50g
- Coconut oil 1tsp

## Steps
1. Dry roast each of the above ingredients on low flame (except for the chillies)
2. Fry the chillies in oil
3. Wait for all the ingredients to cool down
4. Blend together

Prep Time: 30m
Servings: 3 months
